##[#unordered] Unordered

Asterisks tight:

[#list1]
*	asterisk 1
*	asterisk 2
*	asterisk 3


Asterisks loose:

[#list2]
*	asterisk 1

*	asterisk 2

*	asterisk 3

* * *

Pluses tight:

[#list3]
+	Plus 1
+	Plus 2
+	Plus 3


Pluses loose:

[#list4]
+	Plus 1

+	Plus 2

+	Plus 3

* * *


Minuses tight:

[#list5]
-	Minus 1
-	Minus 2
-	Minus 3


Minuses loose:

[#list6]
-	Minus 1

-	Minus 2

-	Minus 3


##[#ordered] Ordered

Tight:

[#list7]
1.	First
2.	Second
3.	Third

and:

[#list8]
1. One
2. Two
3. Three


Loose using tabs:

[#list9]
1.	First

2.	Second

3.	Third

and using spaces:

[#list10]
1. One

2. Two

3. Three

Multiple paragraphs:

[#list11]
1.	Item 1, graf one.

	Item 2. graf two. The quick brown fox jumped over the lazy dog's
	back.
	
2.	Item 2.

3.	Item 3.
